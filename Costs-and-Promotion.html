COSTS

​With current technologies, a singular ticket for one person to be able to visit the International Space Station in low-Earth orbit costs around $55 million dollars. This includes fuel costs and personnel.

​Costs are bound to decrease as new innovations such as the “Starship” being made by SpaceX aims to reduce the payload costs dramatically, making commercial space tourism more accessible to the masses.
