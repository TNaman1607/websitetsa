Introduction

CASA revolutionizes the space tourism industry and aims to make visiting space a feasible means of entertainment and new experiences.

​​Customers adventuring with CASA may see many exquisite sights while orbiting the Earth in LEO, or Low Earth Orbit. This includes satellites, space debris, the International Space Station, and many other sights.
