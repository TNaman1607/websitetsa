LAUNCH​

​All of our launches take place at Kennedy Space Center, a state-of-the-art facility located on Merritt Island, Florida, USA.

​Our launch vehicles are custom-built and designed to ensure the safety and comfort of our passengers. They are equipped with the latest navigation, communication, and safety systems.

​Passengers will be strapped into their seats and given a thorough pre-flight briefing by our experienced mission control team.

RECOVERY

​Our spacecrafts are designed to make a smooth and safe landing back on Earth.

​After landing, passengers will be transported to a nearby recovery facility where they will undergo a medical check-up and debriefing.

​The medical check-up will include an examination of vital signs, as well as any necessary treatments for any issues caused by the microgravity environment.

​All passengers will receive a certificate of completion and a souvenir of their spaceflight.
